High school

To be as coherent as possible, and to make the link with my current training, I'm going to start with high school. During all my previous school years, I always had a taste for science in general, and this, logically, stayed with me until high school, so the 3 options I logically chose were :

Maths

Maths was therefore the most logical and important option in my eyes for further study in the sciences.

Physics-Chemistry

In the same way as for maths, physics-chemistry was essential for me.

Engineering sciences

And finally, my 3rd option, which I unfortunately had to give up in Terminale (final year of high school), enabled me to discover the engineering professions, and in particular IT.

The "BUT"

After these years of high school, I needed to choose a course of further study, still in the sciences, so I had several options. Not wanting to continue with purely theoretical studies such as a preparatory or bachelor's degree, I looked for a course offering a lot of practical work too. And that's when the computer system technology / networking "BUT" turned out to be my favorite choice, as IT had always been the field I wanted to go into, so now I finally had the chance to get involved in it, and so that's why I joined this course, which I really like.

Futures plans

I now have several choices: either I continue my studies, or I go straight into the world of work.
Well, that will depend above all on my future successes, and my progress.
